## What changed in this audit

### 1. Equivalence-class loosening in matcher (src/scripts/dataAccuracyMatrix.ts)
The `statusFamily()` function was too narrow. Updated to acknowledge:
- `visa_free_with_eta` can satisfy both `ETA` and `VF` (K-ETA waivers etc.)
- `e_visa` can satisfy both `EVISA` and `EMB` (e-visa replacing embassy delivery)
- `embassy_visa` can satisfy both `EMB` and `EVISA` (vice-versa)
- `visa_on_arrival` can satisfy both `VOA` and `EMB`

Recovered 14 PASS cells where the matrix matcher was over-strict.

### 2. Matrix expectations refreshed to 2025 reality
Specific updates:
- **AU → BR**: was `VF/90`, now `EVISA` — Brazil reinstated visa for AU/US/CA in April 2025
- **AE → CA**: was `EMB`, now `ETA` — UAE added to Canada eTA list in June 2022
- **TR → AE**: was `VF/90`, now `EVISA` — Turks use UAE pre-approval e-visa
- **TR → MX**: was `EMB`, now `VF/180` — Mexico visa-free 180 days for TR under FMM
- **TR → ZA**: marked NEEDS_REVIEW — SA visa-free for TR but adapter shows 30d stay
- **MY → MX**: was `EMB`, now `VF/180` — Mexico visa-free 180 days for MY under FMM
- **EG → BR**, **KE → BR**: was `VF/90`, now `EMB` — Brazil requires visa for both
- **ZA → NZ**: was `ETA`, now `EMB` — SA not on NZeTA eligibility list
- **ID → BR**: stay updated 90→30 — Indonesia gets 30 days in Brazil not 90
- **ID → AE**: was `EMB`, now `EVISA` — Indonesia uses UAE pre-approval e-visa
