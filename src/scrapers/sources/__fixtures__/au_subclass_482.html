Skills in Demand visa (subclass 482)

The Skills in Demand visa lets sponsoring employers fill skill gaps in their business by hiring skilled workers. It replaced the subclass 457 and Temporary Skill Shortage (TSS) visas.

Streams

There are three streams: Specialist Skills, Core Skills, and Essential Skills.

Income threshold

For the Core Skills stream, you must be paid at least the Core Skills Income Threshold of AUD 73,150 per year, or the annual market salary rate, whichever is higher.

Stay

Up to 4 years.

Cost

From AUD 3,210 application charge for the primary applicant in the Core Skills stream.

Eligibility

  • You must be nominated by an approved sponsor
  • You must have at least 1 year of relevant work experience
  • Your nominated occupation must be on the relevant occupation list
  • You must meet skills, English, health and character requirements
