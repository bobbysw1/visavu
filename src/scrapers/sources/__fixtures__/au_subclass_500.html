Student visa (subclass 500)

The Student visa (subclass 500) lets you stay in Australia for up to 5 years to study at an educational institution.

About this visa

You must be enrolled in a course of study with a provider that is registered on the Commonwealth Register of Institutions and Courses for Overseas Students (CRICOS).

Cost

From AUD 1,600 application charge for the primary applicant. Additional applicants pay separately.

Stay

Up to 5 years, in line with the duration of your course.

Requirements

  • You meet the Genuine Student (GS) requirement
  • You hold Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC) for the period of your stay
  • You have enough money to support yourself: AUD 24,505 in living costs over 12 months for a single applicant, plus tuition and travel
  • You meet English language requirements set by your education provider
  • You meet health and character requirements

Work

You may work up to 48 hours per fortnight while your course is in session, and unlimited hours during scheduled course breaks.
