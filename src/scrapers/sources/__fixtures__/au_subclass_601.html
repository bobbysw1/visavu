Electronic Travel Authority (subclass 601)

The Electronic Travel Authority (ETA) is a digitally-stored visa for short-term tourism or business visits to Australia. Apply via the Australia ETA app.

Cost

There is no application charge but there is a service charge of AUD 20.

Stay

3 months on each arrival within a 12-month period.

Eligible passports

Brunei, Canada, Hong Kong, Japan, Malaysia, Singapore, South Korea, Taiwan, USA.
