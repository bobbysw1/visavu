Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA)

An Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA) is required for visa-exempt foreign nationals to fly to, or transit through, Canada. The cost is CAD $7 and most approvals are returned within minutes.

Validity

An eTA is valid for up to 5 years or until the linked passport expires, whichever comes first. It allows multiple visits of up to 6 months each.

Who needs an eTA

Travellers from visa-exempt countries flying to or transiting through Canada. US citizens do not need an eTA. Travellers entering Canada by land or sea do not need an eTA.
