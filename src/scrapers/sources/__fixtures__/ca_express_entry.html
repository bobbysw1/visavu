Express Entry

Express Entry is the main way to immigrate to Canada as a skilled worker. It manages applications for three federal economic immigration programs:

  • Federal Skilled Worker Program
  • Federal Skilled Trades Program
  • Canadian Experience Class

How it works

Submit a profile that ranks you in the Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS). Highest-ranked candidates are issued an Invitation to Apply (ITA) for permanent residence in periodic draws.

Federal Skilled Worker eligibility

  • At least 1 year of continuous full-time skilled work experience in NOC TEER 0, 1, 2, or 3
  • Language test results (English or French) at CLB 7 or above
  • Educational Credential Assessment for foreign education
  • Pass the minimum 67/100 points on the FSW grid

Fees

Processing fee CAD 950 for the principal applicant. Right of Permanent Residence Fee (RPRF) CAD 575. Biometrics CAD 85.

Processing time

Most applications are processed in 6 months from receipt of a complete application.
