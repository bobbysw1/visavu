CARICOM — Caribbean Community

The CARICOM Single Market and Economy (CSME) provides free movement of CARICOM nationals among member states for up to 6 months on arrival, with skilled-national provisions for residence and work.

CSME Member States

Antigua and Barbuda, Barbados, Belize, Dominica, Grenada, Guyana, Jamaica, Saint Kitts and Nevis, Saint Lucia, Saint Vincent and the Grenadines, Suriname, Trinidad and Tobago.
