Free Movement of Skills (CSME)

The CARICOM Single Market and Economy permits Skilled Nationals to live and work in any of the 12 participating CSME member states without a work permit. A Skilled National Certificate is issued by the destination Member State after a successful application.

Eligible categories

  • University graduates
  • Media workers
  • Sportspersons
  • Artists and musicians
  • Nurses and teachers
  • Artisans, agricultural workers, security guards, beauticians, barbers, hairdressers, cosmetologists, household domestic workers, and other categories added by COTED decisions
