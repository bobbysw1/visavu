The EU Blue Card

The EU Blue Card is a residence permit for non-EU nationals with a recognised university degree and a concrete job offer in Germany.

Salary requirements

You must earn at least €45,300 gross per year in regular professions, or €41,041.62 in shortage occupations.

Stay

The Blue Card is granted for up to 4 years. After 33 months you may apply for a settlement permit (21 months with B1 German).

Application

Apply at the German embassy or consulate in your home country. The application fee is €100; the residence-permit issuance fee is a further €100.
