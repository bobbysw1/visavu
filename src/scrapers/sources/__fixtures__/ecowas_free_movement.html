ECOWAS — Economic Community of West African States

The 15 member states of ECOWAS are committed to the free movement of persons, the right of residence, and the right of establishment, under the Protocol on Free Movement of Persons (1979) and its supplementary protocols.

Member States

Benin, Burkina Faso, Cabo Verde, Côte d'Ivoire, The Gambia, Ghana, Guinea, Guinea-Bissau, Liberia, Mali, Niger, Nigeria, Senegal, Sierra Leone, Togo.

Phases

Phase I — visa-free entry for 90 days; Phase II — right of residence; Phase III — right of establishment.
