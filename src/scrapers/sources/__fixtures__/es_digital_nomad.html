Visa for international teletrabajador (Digital Nomad)

Spain offers a national visa for international remote workers under the Startups Law (Ley 28/2022). The Digital Nomad visa allows non-EU/EEA citizens to live in Spain while working remotely for non-Spanish employers or as freelancers serving non-Spanish clients.

Eligibility

  • Employment relationship with a non-Spanish company for at least 3 months prior to application
  • Demonstrate that your work can be performed remotely
  • Income at or above 200% of the Spanish minimum interprofessional wage (SMI) — approximately €31,752 per year
  • University degree or 3+ years of professional experience
  • Private health insurance valid in Spain
  • Clean criminal record

Duration

Initial visa is granted for 1 year and can be renewed in Spain for 3 more years, then for a further 2 years (5 years total).

Fees

National visa application fee: €80. Foreign Identification Card (TIE) issuance: €16.
