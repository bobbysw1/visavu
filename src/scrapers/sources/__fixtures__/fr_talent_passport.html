Talent Passport (Passeport Talent)

The Talent Passport is a multi-year residence permit for skilled non-EU/EEA professionals coming to France for employment, research, business creation, or artistic / scientific activities.

Eligible categories

  • Highly-qualified employee — Master's degree or equivalent, with annual salary at or above 1.5× SMIC (~€41,933)
  • Salaried employee of a French innovative company
  • Researcher with hosting agreement
  • Investor — minimum €300,000 in tangible French assets and creation of ≥3 jobs
  • Founder of an innovative company recognized by the French state
  • Recognized artist / performer
  • Athlete or coach of international standing

Duration

Up to 4 years on initial grant. Renewable. Eligible for a 10-year resident card or French citizenship after 5 years of residence.

Family

Spouses and minor children receive the Talent (family) permit, which permits work.

Fees

Long-stay visa application: €99. Residence permit (titre de séjour) issuance: €200.
