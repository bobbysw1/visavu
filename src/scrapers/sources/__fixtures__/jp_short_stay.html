Short-stay visa exemption arrangements

Japan grants visa-free short-stay entry to nationals of approximately 70 countries and regions. The maximum permitted period of stay is generally 90 days, though some countries are limited to 30 days (Thailand, Indonesia) or 14 days (Brunei).

Conditions

  • Travel for tourism, short-term business activities, visiting family/friends, or transit
  • No employment or paid activity permitted on this exemption
  • Possess a valid passport for the duration of stay
  • Possess an onward or return ticket and sufficient funds
