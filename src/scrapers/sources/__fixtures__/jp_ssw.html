Specified Skilled Worker (特定技能 / Tokutei Ginō)

The Specified Skilled Worker visa was created in April 2019 to admit foreign workers with adequate skills and Japanese language ability into 14 (now 16) sectors experiencing labour shortages.

Two tiers

  • SSW(i) — up to 5 years total stay, no family sponsorship
  • SSW(ii) — no upper limit on renewals, family sponsorship permitted

Sectors

Care, building cleaning, manufacturing, construction, shipbuilding, automobile maintenance, aviation, accommodation, agriculture, fishery, food and beverages manufacturing, food service, road transport, forestry, wood, and others.

Application

Candidates must pass a sector-specific skill test and a Japanese-language test, then secure employment with a host organisation. The Immigration Services Agency issues a Certificate of Eligibility (COE).
