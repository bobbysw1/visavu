Skilled Migrant Category Resident Visa

The Skilled Migrant Category (SMC) is a points-based residency route for skilled workers who can contribute to New Zealand's economy.

The 6-point system

From October 2023, applicants must reach 6 points across: qualifications, NZ-registered occupation experience, or income at or above the median wage. Different paths to 6 points exist.

Income

The current median wage threshold is NZD 31.61 per hour (approximately NZD 65,750 per year on a 40-hour week).

Eligibility

  • Be aged under 56
  • Meet acceptable standard of health and character
  • Meet English-language requirements
  • Submit an Expression of Interest (EOI) — selected applicants receive an Invitation to Apply

Cost

Application fee: NZD 5,810 (offshore). Immigration Levy: NZD 480. Total NZD 6,290.
