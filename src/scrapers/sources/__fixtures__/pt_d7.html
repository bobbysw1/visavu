Portuguese national visas

Portugal issues several long-stay national visas, including the popular D7 visa for individuals with stable, regular passive income such as pensions, rental income, dividends or royalties.

D7 — Passive Income / Residence visa

Available to non-EU/EEA citizens who can demonstrate sufficient and stable passive income and intend to reside in Portugal.

Income requirement

The minimum income is 100% of the Portuguese minimum wage (approximately €820 per month for the primary applicant), plus 50% for a spouse and 30% for each additional dependent.

Stay

The D7 visa is issued for 4 months and allows two entries to Portugal. Once in Portugal, holders apply at AIMA (formerly SEF) for a residence permit valid 2 years, renewable for a further 3.
