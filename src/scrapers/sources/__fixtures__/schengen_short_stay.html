Regulation (EU) 2018/1806 of the European Parliament and of the Council

listing the third countries whose nationals must be in possession of visas when crossing the external borders and those whose nationals are exempt from that requirement (codification)

Short-stay visa policy

This Regulation sets out which third countries' nationals require a visa to enter the Schengen Area for short stays (up to 90 days in any 180-day period), and which are exempt.

Annex I

List of third countries whose nationals must be in possession of visas when crossing the external borders.

Annex II

List of third countries whose nationals are exempt from the visa requirement when crossing the external borders for stays of no more than 90 days in any 180-day period.

This consolidated text reflects amendments through May 2024.
