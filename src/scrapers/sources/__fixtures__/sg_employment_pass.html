Employment Pass

The Employment Pass (EP) allows foreign professionals, managers and executives to work in Singapore. Candidates must earn a fixed monthly salary at or above the qualifying salary and meet COMPASS framework requirements.

Qualifying salary

The minimum qualifying monthly salary is $5,600 for the general sector and $6,200 for the financial services sector. Older candidates have higher thresholds.

COMPASS

From September 2023, EP applicants must score at least 40 points on the COMPASS framework, which evaluates salary, qualifications, diversity and skills bonus.

Validity

An EP is valid for up to 2 years on first issuance and can be renewed for up to 3 years.

Cost

Application: $105. Issuance: $225 if approved.
