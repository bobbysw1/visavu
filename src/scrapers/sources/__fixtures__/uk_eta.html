Apply for an Electronic Travel Authorisation (ETA)

An Electronic Travel Authorisation (ETA) is a digital permission to travel to the UK. It costs £16 and is required for non-visa nationals visiting the UK for up to 6 months.

Who needs an ETA

You need an ETA if you do not need a visa for short stays in the UK and you are not a British or Irish citizen. The ETA scheme is being rolled out by nationality across 2024 and 2025.

How long it lasts

An ETA is valid for 2 years (or until your passport expires) and allows multiple visits of up to 6 months each.

Apply

Apply online through the UK ETA app or on gov.uk. Most decisions are made within 3 working days.
