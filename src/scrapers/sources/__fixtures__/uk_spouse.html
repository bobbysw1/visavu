UK family visa: partner or spouse

You may be able to apply for a UK family visa if you have a partner who is a British citizen or has settled in the UK.

Eligibility

You must be 18 or over and in a genuine relationship with your partner. Unmarried partners must have lived together for at least 2 years.

Financial requirement

Your partner must be earning at least £29,000 per year. From early 2025, the minimum income requirement will rise to £38,700.

How long it lasts

Your initial grant is for 33 months, and you can apply to extend for a further 30 months. After 5 years cumulative residence on this route, you may apply for indefinite leave to remain.

Fees

The application fee outside the UK is £1,938. You'll also pay the Immigration Health Surcharge of £1,035 per year.

Knowledge of English

You must demonstrate English at CEFR level A1 on initial application, A2 at extension, and B1 at indefinite leave to remain.
