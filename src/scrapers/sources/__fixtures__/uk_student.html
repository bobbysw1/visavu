Student visa

You can apply for a Student visa to study in the UK if you're 16 or over and you have been offered a place on a course by a licensed student sponsor.

Eligibility

  • have an unconditional offer of a place on a course with a licensed student sponsor
  • have enough money to support yourself and pay for your course
  • can speak, read, write and understand English
  • have consent from your parents if you're 16 or 17

Money

You'll usually need to have enough money to pay for your course (for one academic year) plus £1,483 per month for living costs in London or £1,136 per month outside London, for up to 9 months.

How long it lasts

Up to 5 years for degree-level courses, or 2 years for below-degree-level courses.

Fees

The application fee outside the UK is £490. You'll also pay the Immigration Health Surcharge of £776 per year.

Knowledge of English

You must prove your knowledge of English at CEFR level B2 for degree-level courses or B1 for below degree-level.

Work

You may be able to work up to 20 hours per week during term time.
