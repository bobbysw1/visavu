H-1B Specialty Occupations

The H-1B classification applies to people who wish to perform services in a specialty occupation. The petitioning employer must file Form I-129 with USCIS.

Cap

The H-1B is subject to an annual cap of 65,000 visas, plus an additional 20,000 reserved for individuals with a master's degree or higher from a U.S. institution. A lottery is held in March each year to select cap-subject petitions.

Length of stay

An initial H-1B period of stay may be granted for up to 3 years, and may be extended for a total of 6 years (or longer in conjunction with a pending green card application).

Filing

The petitioning employer files Form I-129. Premium Processing (Form I-907) is available for an additional fee with a 15-day adjudication guarantee.
