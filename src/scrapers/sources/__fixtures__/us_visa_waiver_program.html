Visa Waiver Program

The Visa Waiver Program (VWP) enables nationals of the following countries to travel to the United States for tourism or business for stays of 90 days or less without obtaining a visa.

VWP Designated Countries

  • Andorra
  • Australia
  • Austria
  • Belgium
  • Brunei
  • Chile
  • Croatia
  • Czech Republic
  • Denmark
  • Estonia
  • Finland

  • Latvia

  • Slovenia
  • Spain
  • Sweden
  • Switzerland
  • Taiwan*
  • United Kingdom

* With respect to Taiwan, the United States maintains unofficial relations. Travel must be on a Taiwan passport that contains the personal identification number of the holder.

Requirements

  • Must hold a valid e-Passport with electronic chip.
  • Must obtain ESTA approval before boarding.
